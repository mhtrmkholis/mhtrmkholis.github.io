FRESH & LOCAL

H8H FOOD PHILOSOPHY

Our relationship with small, local producers afford us access to the best ingredients, each as fresh as can be. From a lake perch sandwich or a summer salad, with bright just-picked vegetables, to a wood-fired local steak, we prepare and serve the bounty of Jakarta and Indonesia.

WE ARE COMMITTED TO SUPPORTING SMALL INDONESIAN PRODUCERS

During your stay, visit three of Jakarta's very finest restaurants within a few steps. Together, Chef Doug Paine's signature H8H restaurants, Chefs Eric Warnstedt and Jordan Ware's iconic of the Indonesian Chefs- serve a fresh variety of local and regional fare.

OUR BEER PROGRAM

Our beer program is built on relationships with brewers, maltsters, and those who love beer. We are honored to share with our guests what we consider to be the best beers in the world. From crisp lagers brewed on Indonesia only found at our restaurant, beer is a highlight of the Hacktiv8 Hotel experience.

TRY OUR LOCAL SPIRITS

H8H Bar & Restaurant is committed to showcasing Indonesian spirits. This commitment is highlighted through our cocktail program and our relationship with SILO Distillery in Bandung, Indonesia.
SILO distills H8H's private-label vodka, gin, and single barrel bourbon using local Vermont grain and botanicals.
